Flan Recipe

My family's recipe for a smaller sized flan. This recipe is used by many people. Flan is very yummy.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Pour sugar in a pan over medium heat and allow the sugar to melt until it's a brown liquid consistency
  3. Pour brown syrup into a baking dish
  4. In a bowl, mix eggs, follow by beating condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract
  5. Pour mixture into baking dish and cover the dish with aluminum foil
  6. Bake for an hour
  7. When done, flip baking dish over and transfer flan onto a plate

This is my favorite recipe because I have such a sweet tooth. I love baking and eating desserts. Flan is unique and delicious!
